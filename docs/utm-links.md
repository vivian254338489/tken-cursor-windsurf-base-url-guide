## Disclosure language

Use wording like:

```text
TKEN is included as one disclosed example endpoint. Replace it with your chosen provider's documented endpoint, model name, and key.
```

Avoid wording like:

```text
Cursor and Windsurf support all OpenAI-compatible providers.
```

That kind of statement is too broad because support can vary by product version and settings surface.
